About the artist

Soulja is a Sudanese artist who burst onto the scene in 2017 with his mixtape "Aka Soulja". After spending a few years in the USA, he returned to Sudan and established himself as a key figure in the country's rising hip-hop scene. His music blends elements of Sudanese culture and a variety of rap styles, earning him recognition and acclaim throughout the Middle East and North Africa. Currently residing in Cairo, Soulja is signed to SVNBIRDS where he released his debut album “The Top Freestyle” in collaboration with a number of up and coming artists and producers from the region, which has been featured on Spotify and Apple Music playlists and garnered significant press from the likes of SceneNoise and OkayAfrica. He is currently working on his sophomore album, due to be released in 2024.
