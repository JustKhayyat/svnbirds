77 is an Egyptian Grammy-recognized producer, executive producer, and founder of SVNBIRDS, shaping the sound of Arabic hip-hop. Known for his dark, hard-hitting, and atmospheric production, he blends gritty trap elements with cinematic textures, creating a signature sound that has defined the MENA rap scene. He has executive-produced Soulja’s album, with Soulja & Montiyago, and EP with Uzu, all of which topped Egyptian and GCC charts, amassing millions of streams. With over 100K monthly Spotify listeners, 77 is the most-streamed hip-hop producer in the GCC.
